Sunny Good Morning

Your alarm finally makes you get up.

Sunny pairs your alarm with an NFC sticker across the room. When it rings, the fastest way out is to stand up and scan the sticker.

Download on the App Store See how it works

Built for real mornings

Regular alarms are too easy to ignore.

Sunny is for 8 AM lectures, early shifts, gym plans, and every alarm you swear you will not snooze. Put the sticker far enough away that you have to leave bed.

Before Sunny 8 alarms. 3 snoozes. Late again.
After Sunny Alarm rings. You get up. Morning starts.

How it works

One sticker. One job.

Sunny keeps the flow simple so the morning decision is already made.

01

Set the alarm

Choose the time, tone, repeat days, and alarm volume inside Sunny.

02

Place the sticker

Pair an NFC sticker and put it across the room, near a door, desk, or bathroom mirror.

03

Scan to stop

When the alarm rings, Sunny stays on until the right sticker is tapped.

Inside the app

A warm interface with a stubborn little sun.

The app tracks wake-ups, streaks, and Sunny's growth. The design is friendly, but the alarm flow is built around physical action.
